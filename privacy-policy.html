Privacy Policy — Guwahati Bus Helper

Last updated: 6 July 2026

Guwahati Bus Helper ("the app") is developed by Ritom Puzari. This policy explains what data the app uses. The short version: the app has no accounts, no ads, no analytics, and stores nothing about you on any server.

Data the app does NOT collect

The app does not require sign-in and does not collect, store, or share your name, email address, contacts, photos, files, device identifiers, or usage analytics. Bus route searches you make in the app are processed entirely on your device and never leave it.

Location (optional, off by default)

The app contains an optional, experimental "Nearby stops" feature which is switched off by default. If you enable it in the app's About settings and grant the location permission, the app:

  • reads your device location to sort bus stoppages by distance on your device;
  • sends your approximate coordinates over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection to the OpenStreetMap Overpass API (overpass-api.de) solely to fetch bus stops mapped near you.

Your location is used only at that moment ("ephemeral processing"). It is never stored by the app, never linked to your identity, and never used for advertising or profiling. If you keep the feature off or deny the permission, the app never accesses your location at all.

Map tiles

When the optional map view is opened, map images are loaded from OpenStreetMap tile servers. Like any web request, this transmits your IP address to those servers. See the OpenStreetMap privacy policy.

Google Maps hand-off

The "Navigate in Google Maps" button opens the Google Maps app or website on your device. Anything that happens there is governed by Google's privacy policy, not this one.

Permissions summary

  • INTERNET / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — map tiles and nearby-stop lookup only; core route planning works fully offline.
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION — only used if you enable the optional Nearby feature.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and collects no personal data from any user.
